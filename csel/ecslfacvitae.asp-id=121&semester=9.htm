Curriculum vitae
Professor of international law and Director, Maastricht Centre for Human Rights (since 2000). Vice-Dean of the Maastricht Faculty of Law (since 2008). (Senior) Lecturer in International Law at Erasmus University Rotterdam (1987-2000). Legal Adviser and UN Representative in New York of the International Secretariat of Amnesty International (1978-1987). Master degrees in international law and international relations from Groningen University (1973) and the Fletcher School of Law and Diplomacy (1974). Ph.D. from Leiden University (1990). Chairs the Dutch Government’s Advisory Committee on International Law and is a member of the Netherlands Government’s Advisory Committee on Human Rights. Member of the Board of Editors of the Netherlands International Law Review. Trustee of the Business and Human Rights Resource Centre.

Main publications

The Impact of Human Rights Law on General International Law, ed., with M. Scheinin (Oxford University Press: 2009). Extraterritorial Application of Human Rights Treaties, ed., with F. Coomans (Intersentia: 2004). Inaugural lecture: De humanisering van het volkenrecht [The Humanization of International Law] (Universitaire Pers Maastricht: 2001). Liability of Multinational Corporations under International Law, ed., with S. Zia-Zarifi (Kluwer Law International: 2000). Volkenrechtelijke aansprakelijkheid van de staat jegens het individu [State Responsibility Towards the Individual], Mededelingen van de Nederlandse Vereniging voor Internationaal Recht No. 115 (1997) 5-49. Ph.D. dissertation: Inter-State Accountability for Violations of Human Rights (University of Pennsylvania Press: 1992).

Expertise

General international law, international dispute settlement, international human rights law, international humanitarian law.
