2011-2012学年欧洲-国际法第九单元二次补考试题
发布于  2012/6/30 8:24:17
第九单元二次补考试题：“国际经济法”(International Economic Law)

Title of Course: WTO Law
Name of Lecturer: Dr. Denise Prévost
Instructions:
This second resit exam consists of 3 theoretical questions. All three questions have to be answered.
You may use any of the WTO Agreements during the exam.
Always support your answer with legal arguments and references to the relevant articles of WTO agreements, WTO practice and case law where appropriate.

Question 1:
Institutional Aspects of the WTO

The WTO has been called a ‘member-driven’ organisation. Please explain this term by reference to the composition and decision making procedures of WTO organs and the role and function of the WTO Secretariat and Director General in the WTO.

Question 2:
WTO Dispute Settlement

Set out and describe briefly the four main steps in WTO dispute settlement procedures. In your answer, please address also the preference for mutually agreed solutions to disputes, and the problems that small economies have in using the enforcement mechanism.

Question 3:
Substantive WTO Law

The rules of the WTO try to balance the need for trade liberalisation, on the one hand, and the importance of allowing WTO Members enough space to pursue important policy objectives (such as environmental protection, security, protection against unfair trade or economic emergencies) on the other hand. Please choose one area where this balance is reflected, and discuss the applicable rules in that specific area.
