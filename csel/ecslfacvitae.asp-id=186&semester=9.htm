Liling Yue
Professor of Law
China University of Political Science and Law

Education: China University of Political Science and Law, LL.B, LL.M, Ph.D.

Professional Experience: Fulbright scholar, University of California, Davis, 2010-2011;Visiting and Lecturing Professor at Harvard University, Sam Houston State University, March 2011,Inter American University of Puerto Rico, July 2011.Visiting and lecturing Professor, University of California, Davis, 2008; Visiting Professor, Cambridge University Criminology Institute, 2003; Visiting Scholar at Danish Institute for Human Rights, Copenhagen, Denmark,2000; Alexander von Humboldt Scholar, Max-Planck-Institute for Foreign and International Criminal Law, 1995-1996,many other times; International Visitor Program on U.S. Judiciary and the Rule of Law, 1998, in U.S. Visiting scholar at the University of British Columbia,1994, Vancouver, Canada; Visiting scholar at United Nations Interregional Crime and Justice Research Institute ( UNICRI ), 1991,Italy. Has delivered lectures in foreign universities and courts in Belgium, Germany, South Korea, Slovenia, South Africa, Switzerland, United Kingdom, United States and Vietnam. Served as leader of several research projects, such as EU-China comparative research on sentencing; Effective criminal defense and criminal trial and human rights protection, and Participated some important legislative projects, such as criminal procedure law reform from 1994- 1996.

Courses Taught: Criminal Procedure, Comparative Criminal Procedure, Comparative Criminal Justice, Criminal Justice and Human Rights Protection, German Criminal Justice, Implementation of Human Rights Law, and International Human Rights. English language courses for foreign students: Chinese Criminal Law, Chinese Criminal Procedure Law, and Chinese Criminal Justice.
Courses at China-EU Law School: Criminal Justice in China
