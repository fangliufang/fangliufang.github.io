-Date of Birth : 18th of March 1973
-Nationality : Spanish
-Contact Details : ignacio.tirado@uam.es/itirado@worldbank.org

-Current Position : Currently, Ignacio Tirado is Special Counsel at the World Bank’s Legal Department (Private Sector), and Professor (Profesor Titular) of Corporate and Insolvency Law at the Universidad Autónoma of Madrid (Spain). A qualified lawyer, Ignacio was Of Counsel and co-director of the Business Restructuring and Insolvency Practice of Hogan Lovells, LLP (Madrid Office, then Lovells LLP), until he joined the World Bank. Ignacio’s research interests (present and former) as well as legal practice focuses on Insolvency Law, Corporate Law (business and company restructuring) and securities regulation.
