 首页  教授  Wolfgang Werner Däubler 
Wolfgang Werner Däubler
德国
中国政法大学中欧法学院杰出访问教授
简历 课程 论文 演讲
Present position: Retired Professor of German and European Labour Law, Civil Law and Economic Law
    
Specific experience in China
Teaching legal terminology during 3 months at Bei Wai in Beijing in 2006, at the same time teaching civil law at the Chinese University for Politics and Law; visiting (“consulting”) professor at the CDHK, Tongji University Shanghai in 2006 and 2007;
Cons ultant of the Labour Ministry during the elaboration of the labour contract law in 2006 and 2007;
Teaching one month at CESL in 2011 and in spring 2012 (“Comparative Labour Law”).

Professional experience
Professor at the University of Bremen since 1971;
Visiting Professor at the University of Paris X (Nanterre) in 1987 and 1990;
Visiting Professor at the University of Bordeaux in 1994 and at the University of Antwerp in 1996;
Visiting Professor at the University of Trento in 2006 and 2007;
Member of the supervisory board of the State Bank of Bremen;
Giving opinions to trade unions, works councils, foundations and enterprises mostly on questions of labour law;
Consulting activities to governments and labour ministries in Slovenia(1998C2002), Kyrgistan(1994C1995), Vietnam(2002C2012) and Mongolia(2011-2012);
Seminars on labour relations in different countries of Latin America since 1988, especially in Brazil.

Publications
Some 20 books, e.g.
Deguo Guyuan Quanyi De Weihu, Gongren-Publishing House, Beijing 2009, 157 pages;
BGB-Kompakt, 3. Aufl. München (dtv) 2008;
Das Arbeitsrecht, Band 1, 15. Aufl. 2006 , Band 2, 12. Aufl. 2009;
(Spanish translation of both volumes published in 1995);
Gewerkschaftsrechte im Betrieb, 11. Aufl., Neuwied 2010;
Kommentar zum Kündigungsschutzrecht (mit Kittner und Zwanzinger), Aufl. Frankfurt/Main 2011;
Kommentar zum Betriebsverfassungsgesetz (mit Kittner, Klebe u.a.), 13. Aufl. Frankfurt/Main 2012;
Tarifvertragsrecht, Kommentar, 3. Aufl., Baden-Baden 2012;
Kommentar zum Allgemeinen Gleichbehandlungsgesetz, 2. Aufl. Baden-Baden 2008.

Numerous articles especially on labour law, on privatization problems and the liability of rating agencies
