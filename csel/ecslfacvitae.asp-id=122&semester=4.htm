Education

Ph. D. Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, 1997.

Academic and Professional Experience

Professor of Legal Philosophy, Legal Theory, Ethics and Human Rights and Bioethics. Departament of Legal Philosophy. Law School, Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (2001-present).
Professor of the Stanford University Program in Madrid (2007-present). Courses: "Issues in Bioethics Across Cultures", "Health Care: A contrastive analysis between Spain and the US".
Visiting Professor at the School of Law of the Universitat Pompeu Fabra de Barcelona (January-march, 2005).
Vice-dean of the Law School, Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (june 2003-present).
Member of the Bioethics Advisory Comittee of the Hospital de la Princesa, Madrid.
Recipient of the postdoctoral scholarship sponsored by the regional government of Madrid (1998-2001).
Recipient of the B.F.P.I scholarship sponsored by the regional government of Madrid (1993-1997).
Visiting Researcher, New York University (august 2004).
Visiting Researcher, Balliol College, Oxford University (fall semester, 2000).
Visiting Researcher, Northwestern University, Chicago (fall semester, 1996).
Visiting Researcher, University of California at Berkeley (fall semester, 1995).
Visiting Researcher and postgraduate student, Syracuse University (1993-94).
Part-time instructor of Spanish, Syracuse University, Department of Foreign Languages (1993-1994).
