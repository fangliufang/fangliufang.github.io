HUGHES-CASTELL is hiring in Shanghai and Beijing
发布于  2012/9/1 18:51:50
Do you like helping people? We are a specialist international recruitment consultancy with offices in Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing and Singapore.  We place lawyers and compliance professionals into multinational corporations and international law firms in China and the rest of Asia.  We are currently seeking to recruit outgoing, dynamic and self-motivated professionals who enjoy guiding and advising clients and candidates. If you love dealing with people, have strong communication skills and have strong intuition and business sense, if you think you would enjoy guiding people’s careers and being respected as a knowledgeable professional, come and talk to us and learn more about becoming a recruitment consultant. You must be a university graduate with a degree in law, business, marketing or psychology. We offer competitive salaries, bonuses and incentives.  You must have the highest integrity and ethics, be entrepreneurial, with good business sense and a sincere motivation to help people. Fluency in English is a must as is a positive attitude and a pro-active approach. Interested applicants should send their resume in Chinese and English to lchen@hughes-castell.com.hk

公司简介

HUGHES-CASTELL为一家面向全球的法律人才咨询公司，分别在香港、上海、北京、新加坡设立分支机构，在亚洲法律人才咨询领域始终保持行业领先地位。HUGHES-CASTELL不但是香港、新加坡、中国内地的行业先锋，业务更遍及韩国、日本、印度、越南和马来西亚等亚太国家和地区。 HUGHES-CASTELL客户行业涵盖广泛，遍及高新技术产业、金融、房地产以及建筑工程、能源、通讯、工业制造、媒体娱乐业、医疗机构、综合性行业及专业组织等。客户群主要包括提供专业法律服务的跨国律师事务所和本地律师事务所，及包括世界500强企业在内的全球性的跨国公司和本地企业。近年来，随着中国国际化进程的加速，HUGHES-CASTELL面向中国内地客户的业务发展迅速，并建立和积累了广泛的客户关系。作为一家赋有社会责任感的企业，我们不只为客户提供招聘求职服务，更力求在保证为客户提供优质服务的同时，全力维护求职者的权益，从而促成劳资各方实现共赢。

网址：Hughes-Castell.com.hk
