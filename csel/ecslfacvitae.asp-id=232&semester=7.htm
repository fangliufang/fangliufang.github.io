Current Practice

- Party-appointed arbitrator on a regular basis at various international arbitration institutions in Europe, including MKIK (Hungary), DIS (Germany) and VIAC (Austria)
- Expert witness on a regular basis at various international arbitration institutions, including ICSID (Washington D.C.) and foreign state courts, including the High Court of Justice (London)
- Attorney at law specializing in litigation and arbitration, Partner of the international law firm KNP LAW Nagy Koppany Varga and Partners in Budapest, Hungary ( www.knplaw.com )
