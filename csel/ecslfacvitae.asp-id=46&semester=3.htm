Brief Introduction

Born in Kortrijk (Belgium) on 22 August 1978
Assistant professor of international law, Leuven University
Cedric Ryngaert (1978) studied law at Leuven University (1996-2001). He obtained his PhD (on jurisdiction in international law) at the same university in 2007, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. J. Wouters. He was visiting researcher at Harvard Law School in 2005, and assistant district prosecutor in Leuven in 2006.
He is acting lecturer at Leuven University since 2006, and assistant professor since 2008. He is also lecturer in international law at the University of Utrecht since 2007.
