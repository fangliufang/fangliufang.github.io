中国政法大学中欧法学院2012年推免生考试面试题（2011年9月24日）
发布于  2011/9/30 15:03:10
1. 如何贴标签？
有三个箱子，一个装的是桃子，一个装的是梨子，还有一个装的是桃子和梨子。箱子的标签都贴错了。你只打开一个箱子，拿出一个水果。看过这个水果后，怎么能给这三个箱子正确地贴上标签？

2. 该不该交学费？
希腊智者普罗太戈拉教一位青年人如何打官司，约定如果他第一次诉讼获胜才交学费，否则分文不取。随后，普罗太戈拉到法院告他，说他欠学费，要其交纳。他该不该交学费？

3. “律师”或“法师”？
英文“law” 中译为法、法律，“律师”一词英文可为“lawyer”，为什么把 “lawyer”译成律师而不是“法官”和“法师”呢？

4. 如何判？
两市民自愿互掷飞刀，结果一人中刀重伤, 法院判掷刀者伤害罪。又有马戏团两艺人互掷飞刀，结果一人中刀重伤, 掷刀者无罪。
你同意否？为什么？

5. 怎样种树?
在一个地方种四棵树, 每两棵树的间隔均相等, 怎样种?

6. 遗产应怎样分配?
一位寡妇．要把她丈夫遗留下来的３，５００元遗产同她即将生产的孩子一起分配。生的如果是儿子，按照当地的法律，做母亲的应分得儿子份额的一半，生的如果是女儿，就应分得女儿份额的两倍。可是事情却是：她生了一对双胞胎――一男一女。那么，遗产应怎样分配才合法呢？

7. 哪些部位需要加固？
二战时英国空军经常派轰炸机去袭击德国。由于德国防空火力较猛，轰炸机损失惨重。为了降低损失，决定给飞机进行加固。条件所限，只能加固飞机的少数部位。然后，英国空军对返航的飞机进行仔细的观察、统计和分析, 发现大多数弹孔集中在飞机机翼；只有少数弹孔位于驾驶舱。
假如你负责此事，你会加固哪个部位呢？

8. 是否犯诈骗罪?
根据某刑法，凡利用人们的无知而使其蒙受损失的则犯诈骗罪。那么, 在考试时, 教师判有的学生不及格, 是否犯诈骗罪?

9. 下午好，约翰逊夫人
约翰逊夫人告一个男子毁坏名誉罪，因为这个男子称她为猪。男子被判有罪。在判决宣布后，他问法官：“这是否意味着，我不能称约翰逊夫人为猪？”法官作了肯定的回答。
“这是否也意味着，我不能称一头猪为‘约翰逊夫人’？”他又问。法官回答，他可以把一头猪称为“约翰逊夫人”，而不用害怕法律。男子听后直看着约翰逊夫人，说：“下午好，约翰逊夫人。”
你同意法官的回答吗?

10. 结婚和当兵
中国法律规定：男人22岁才能结婚，可18岁就能当兵。
这说明了什么?

11. 孩子说了什么?
有一家人, 夫妻俩和一个12岁的孩子, 想租房子, 他们看到一张出租广告, 便跑去询问。温和的房东出来,  丈夫问道：”这房屋出租吗?” 房东看了看他们, 遗憾地说:”啊，实在对不起，我们不租给有孩子的家庭。”他们只好默默地走开。
那孩子思考了一会, 又去敲房东的门。房东又出来了, 他听了那孩子说了一句话之后，高声地笑了起来，决定把房子租给他们一家。那孩子用什么话说服了房东?

12. 西红柿是水果还是蔬菜？
十九世纪末美国法律规定,进口水果免交关税, 而进口蔬菜须交10%的关税. 有一商人进口了一批西红柿, 税务官认为西红柿是蔬菜, 要商人交关税, 商人则认为西红柿是水果,无须交税, 遂发生争议.
你如何评判这一争议?

13. In February 2011 the Beijing Municipal Government Bureau issued a Notice restricting transactions in the real estate market. According to the Notice, households with permanent resident registration in Beijing can buy up to two apartments, whereas households that are temporarily registered in Beijing may buy one apartment if they have paid taxes or social security contributions for five years. Do the measures comply with higher ranking law? What kind of legal remedies are available against the Notice or against decisions taken on the basis of the Notice?

14. The Supreme People's Court ('SPC') introduced a case guiding system in 2010. Under this new system, the SPC selects and publishes court decisions which have to be taken into account by lower courts when they decide similar cases. Discuss if guiding cases have binding effect on lower courts. What are the reasons for the introduction of such a system?

15. Constitutional review denotes a system in which courts or a special court can review the constitutionality of parliamentary legislation. Why do most European states have such a system, where judges can overrule parliament? Why is there no constitutional review in the PRC?

16. Art. 29 of the Supreme People's Court Judicial Interpretation on the Compensation for Personal Injury stipulates that compensation for death shall be calculated for 20 years in light of the per capita disposable income of the urban residents or the per capita net income of the rural residents at the locality of the case-accepting court of the last year. In 2006 a court in Beijing's Chaoyang district awarded compensation to the family members of two victims of a car accident. The family of the victim with a permanent registration in the countryside received 170,000 RMB, whereas the family of the victim who was permanently registered in the city received an amount of 410,000 RMB. Such decisions have been criticised as wrong and discriminatory. What is your view?

17. In 1997 Hong Kong became part of Chinese state territory. The Hong Kong Basic Law, a law enacted by the National People's Congress, states in Article 5 that the socialist system and policies shall not be practiced in Hong Kong, and the previous capitalist system and way of life shall remain unchanged for 50 years. Does Article 5 of the Hong Kong Basic Law comply with the PRC Constitution? Explain the legal relationship between the Constitution and the Hong Kong Basic Law.

18. Explain the principle of freedom of contract. Give examples of provisions of PRC law that embody the principle of freedom of contract.

19. A runs a Photo Art Studio where she sells professional camera equipment and develops films. On the wall behind the counter she has displayed 'standard terms' that apply to all customers: "1. The Studio is not liable for loss or destruction of negatives which have been submitted by customers for enlargement. 2. The Studio has the right to withhold negatives of developed films unless customers order enlargements or pay 15 RMB per negative. 3. The Studio is entitled to search the bags of customers when they leave the Studio." B submitted a film for development in the Studio. He would like to know whether he is contractually bound by the displayed standard terms.

20. A owns a notebook computer and lends it to her friend B. They agree that B can use the computer on a business trip and will return it to A after the trip. During the business trip, B sells the computer to C for 15000 RMB. When C buys the computer, he thinks that B is the owner of it. A becomes very angry when she hears that B has sold the computer. She wants the computer back from B or C or at least compensation for her loss. Examine the claims of A against B and C under PRC law.

21. Most constitutions follow either the parliamentary or the presidential system. Explain the differences and give examples. Is it true that parliamentary systems have a particular strong parliament whereas presidential systems a particular strong president?

22. The implementation of international law on the national level is often described in terms of monism (international and national law form one legal system) or dualism (international law and national law are separate legal systems). Explain the practice in China against the background of those two models.
