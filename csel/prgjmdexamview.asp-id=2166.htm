2012-2013学年欧洲-国际法第五单元考试试题
发布于  2013/3/15 16:56:58
第五单元: 欧盟的法律与政治   Module V: Law and Politics of the EU

Title of Course: European Political System
Name of Lecturer: Professor Carlos Alba
Instructions: Please answer only one question
Question:
A) Political Parties: Types, Functions, Organizations and Problems;
Or
B) The relationships between bureaucrats and elected politicians in the western political systems.


Title of Course: Political and Economic Dimension of the EU
Name of Lecturer: Professor Thomas Eger
Instructions: Please answer all the following questions, relatively short answers to each question are required.
Question 1:
There is a committee with three members. Assume that they have votes according to the following table:


a) Calculate the Shapley-Shubik Index, when a simple majority is necessary to reach the quorum (51 votes required).
b) What happens, when the majority rule is changed to the absolute majority rule (67 votes required)? Recalculate the Shapley-Shubik Index for your answer.

Question 2:
Explain the difference between positive and negative integration and describe the mechanism by which negative integration will eventually lead to positive integration.

Question 3:
What is the importance of the Cassis-de-Dijon decision for European integration.


Title of Course: Legal Foundations of European Union Law (I)
Name of Lecturer: Professor Remco Van Rhee
Question:
Explain the concept of EU Law Supremacy. Illustrate your understanding of this concept by referring to the interplay of EU Law and International Agreements entered into by Member States. When appropriate, refer to relevant TFEU articles and case-law.


Title of Course: Legal Foundations of European Union Law (II)
Name of Lecturer: Mr Karsten Nowrot
Question:
What are the requirements that a natural person (individual) has to fulfill in order to initiate an admissible annulment proceeding at the Court of Justice of the European Union against an act adopted by an EU institution?
